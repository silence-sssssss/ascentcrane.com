The Self-propelled Cherry Picker is engineered for work at height where vertical lifting alone is not enough. The articulated or telescopic boom structure allows the platform to move around piping, shelving, machinery, façade projections, and other obstacles while maintaining operator access. It is widely applied in construction finishing, external maintenance, airport service, commercial facility installation, and large warehouse operations.

## Application Notes

This machine is best for customers who need access over obstacles instead of pure straight-up lifting. It improves working efficiency on complex sites because operators can reposition and elevate from the platform controls without separate transport equipment. Ascent Crane can supply indoor electric, hybrid, or rough-terrain configurations depending on the duty profile.
