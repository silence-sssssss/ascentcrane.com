The Adjustable Height Steel Gantry is built for industrial customers who need stronger capacity, a more rigid structure, and reliable performance under repeated duty. Height-adjustable legs make it easier to pass through doors, work under varying roof elevations, or adapt the crane to different lifting tasks inside the same facility. This model is widely used in fabrication shops, machine maintenance departments, mold handling areas, and project installation work.

## Application Notes

This gantry is recommended when the customer needs a practical balance between mobility and higher lifting capacity. The steel design gives better rigidity for repetitive operations, while the adjustable leg system improves clearance management during transport and setup. Ascent Crane can customize wheel material, paint system, beam section, and height range according to site conditions.
