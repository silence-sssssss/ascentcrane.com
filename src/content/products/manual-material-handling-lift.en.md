The Manual Material Handling Lift is built for facilities that need a simple, low-cost lifting tool for cartons, bins, machine components, display materials, and packaged goods. It bridges the gap between basic hand trucks and larger powered lifting equipment. Because it does not rely on complex electrical systems, it is attractive for retail stores, workshops, laboratories, archives, and maintenance teams that prioritize portability and easy upkeep.

## Application Notes

This lift is ideal where powered stackers would be oversized or too expensive. It provides safe vertical positioning for light industrial tasks without needing battery charging or electrical maintenance. Ascent Crane can adapt fork dimensions, mast height, wheel material, and attachment type to match the handling profile of the end user.
