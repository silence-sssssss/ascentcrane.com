The Electric Hydraulic Scissor Lift is intended for customers who need reliable vertical access in factories, warehouses, malls, installation projects, and facility management operations. It combines stable scissor structure geometry with hydraulic lifting power and simple electric controls, making it suitable for repetitive work at height with lower operator fatigue. Depending on the working environment, the platform can be configured as fixed, semi-mobile, or battery-driven mobile type.
